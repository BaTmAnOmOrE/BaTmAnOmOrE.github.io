Emotional Intelligence

So what is emotional Intelligence?


Emotional Intelligence has 3 main pillars:


#Mindfulness#

#Self knowledge and Mastery#

#Creating Useful Mental Habits#



Emotional Intelligence or EQ is a practice that teaches about having positive emotions so that we have better self-respect and respect for others as well as well as making better choices before reacting. EQ also helps you acheive your personal goals and allows to communicate better and have a greater compassion for others.
